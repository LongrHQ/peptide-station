### Key SEO Principles

1. **Pillar pages + cluster content:** Each major peptide gets a pillar page (comprehensive guide) + 5–10 cluster articles targeting long-tail variations
2. **US-localised content:** Explicitly address US legal context (FDA enforcement, research-use-only), US shipping, US competitors — captures US-specific intent
3. **Research use framing:** Legal necessity AND SEO benefit — "research peptides" gets 9,900–14,800/month US searches; "research chemicals" has volume
4. **E-E-A-T:** Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness — especially important in US health SEO. US-licensed medical advisor bylines, COA links, sourcing transparency
5. **Internal linking:** Tight product-to-content linking (every learn article links to relevant product)
6. **Schema markup:** Product schema, FAQ schema, article schema throughout
7. **US local SEO:** "Peptide therapy near me" gets 6,600–8,100/month — consider location pages for major US metros (Austin, Miami, Denver, LA, NYC)
