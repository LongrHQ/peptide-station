## What Is Peptide Station?

Peptide Station is a new e-commerce and educational platform focused exclusively on **peptide pens** — pre-mixed, auto-injector delivery systems for the full spectrum of therapeutic peptides. It sits under the Longr umbrella but operates as a standalone consumer brand.

The market opportunity: peptide therapeutics is a $46–141B global market (depending on segment scope), growing at 8–10% CAGR. The consumer/biohacker end is dominated by grey-market research chemical vendors with poor UX, confusing product ranges, fake COAs, and zero education. There is a clear gap for a trusted, beautifully designed, education-first brand.

**Peptide Station differentiators:**
- Pen-first delivery (no syringes, no mixing — just dial and inject)
- Education-led: every product comes with a guide; platform has a full content library
- Third-party tested with published COAs (US-accredited labs)
- Compliant framing (research use, US regulatory framework — FDA/DEA aware)
- Longr brand DNA: longevity, science, lifestyle
